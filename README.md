🚀 SyncUp

Find Teammates. Build Projects. Grow Together.

SyncUp is a student collaboration platform designed to help students discover exciting projects, connect with like-minded teammates, and collaborate efficiently. The platform aims to bridge the gap between students with innovative ideas and students with the skills needed to bring those ideas to life.

⸻

📌 Problem Statement

Many students have great project ideas but struggle to find teammates with the right skills and interests. At the same time, many talented students are looking for opportunities to work on meaningful projects but don’t know where to start.

SyncUp solves this problem by creating a centralized platform where students can showcase projects, find collaborators, and communicate in real time.
